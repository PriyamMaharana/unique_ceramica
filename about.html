About Us

Modern & Luxury
A confluence of Class, Quality & Luxury

Unique Ceramica is the name that provide exclusive designs, and superlative tiles quality. Established in 2023, Unique Ceramica is a leading tiles manufacturer and exporter based in India.

We started this journey with the sole aim to produce & deliver premium quality tiles products to the market at affordable rates.We regularly export our ceramic products in many of the cities across India. Unique Ceramica’s strong & dedicated global network of distributors, suppliers, and retailers ensure that our finest products reach the larger public.
